Company FAQ - TechStore Solutions

=== GENERAL INFORMATION ===

Q: What is our return policy?
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products. Items must be in original condition with all packaging and accessories included.

Q: What are your business hours?
A: We are open Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM EST. Weekend support is available via email only.

Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide including Canada, UK, Australia, and most of Europe.

Q: Where is your company located?
A: Our headquarters is located at 123 Technology Drive, San Francisco, CA 94105, United States.

Q: How can I contact customer support?
A: You can reach us at support@techstore.com, call 1-800-TECH-STORE, or use our live chat available 24/7.

=== SHIPPING & DELIVERY ===

Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 days) and overnight shipping are also available.

Q: What are the shipping costs?
A: Standard shipping is free for orders over $50. Orders under $50 have a $5.99 flat rate. Express shipping costs $15.99.

Q: Do you offer free shipping?
A: Yes, we offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50 within the continental United States.

Q: Can I track my order?
A: Yes, you will receive a tracking number via email within 24 hours of your order being shipped.

Q: Do you ship to PO boxes?
A: Yes, we ship to PO boxes for standard shipping only. Express and overnight shipping require a physical address.

Q: What if my package is lost or damaged?
A: Contact us immediately with your order number. We will investigate and either resend or refund your order within 5 business days.

=== PAYMENTS & PRICING ===

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

Q: Is it safe to use my credit card on your site?
A: Yes, we use 256-bit SSL encryption and are PCI DSS compliant. Your payment information is never stored on our servers.

Q: Do you offer financing options?
A: Yes, we partner with Affirm and Klarna to offer buy-now-pay-later options with 0% APR for qualified customers.

Q: Can I use multiple payment methods for one order?
A: Currently, we only accept one payment method per order. You can use gift cards in combination with credit cards.

Q: Do you price match?
A: Yes, we offer price matching for identical products from authorized retailers. Contact us within 7 days of purchase.

=== PRODUCTS & SERVICES ===

Q: What products do you sell?
A: We sell laptops, desktop computers, tablets, smartphones, accessories, software, and consumer electronics.

Q: Do you offer warranties?
A: All products come with manufacturer warranties. We also offer extended warranty plans from 1-3 years for additional coverage.

Q: Can I cancel my order?
A: Yes, you can cancel within 2 hours of placing the order. After that, the order enters processing and cannot be cancelled.

Q: Do you offer technical support?
A: Yes, we offer free technical support for 90 days after purchase. Extended support plans are available for purchase.

Q: Are your products new or refurbished?
A: All our products are brand new unless specifically marked as "Certified Refurbished" in the product title.

=== ACCOUNT & ORDERS ===

Q: Do I need an account to make a purchase?
A: No, guest checkout is available. However, creating an account allows you to track orders and save preferences.

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. You'll receive a password reset link via email within 5 minutes.

Q: Can I modify my order after placing it?
A: Orders can be modified within 1 hour of placement. Contact customer service immediately with your order number.

Q: How do I check my order status?
A: Log into your account and go to "My Orders" or use the tracking link sent to your email.

Q: Can I save items for later?
A: Yes, use the "Add to Wishlist" feature. Wishlist items are saved for 90 days.

=== RETURNS & EXCHANGES ===

Q: How do I return a product?
A: Log into your account, go to "My Orders", select the item, and click "Return Item". Print the prepaid shipping label and drop off at any UPS location.

Q: When will I receive my refund?
A: Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your return. It may take an additional 5-7 days to appear in your account.

Q: Can I exchange a product?
A: Yes, follow the return process and place a new order for the item you want. Exchanges are not directly processed.

Q: What items cannot be returned?
A: Software, gift cards, opened software licenses, and clearance items marked "Final Sale" cannot be returned.

Q: Is there a restocking fee?
A: No restocking fees for returns within 30 days in original condition. Opened items may be subject to a 15% restocking fee.

=== SPECIAL PROGRAMS ===

Q: Do you have a loyalty program?
A: Yes, TechStore Rewards gives you 1 point per dollar spent. 100 points = $5 reward. Members get exclusive deals and early access to sales.

Q: Do you offer student discounts?
A: Yes, verified students get 10% off all purchases. Verify your student status through our Student Discount Program.

Q: Do you have a referral program?
A: Yes, refer a friend and both of you get $20 off your next purchase of $100 or more.
